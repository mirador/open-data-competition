The Mirador Open Data Competition

About

Mirador is a software tool to explore complex datasets and help you find new correlations between variables. We believe that Open Data -the idea that data not only in science, but also in areas like goverment and finance, should be made available to everyone- is a very powerful idea that promotes participation and transparency. So, we put together the Mirador Open Data Competition to hihglight the importance of Open Data and also to give you some rewards for your findings with Mirador!

We packaged four publicly available datasets in health, sports and global development, and you will have one month to explore these datasets with Mirador and submit your findings. A jury of specialists will evaluate the sumbissions and select the 7 top ones based on novelty, significance, and possible impact.

Keep reading to learn the details of the competition, how to register and participate, and the features and operation of Mirador.Thanks for your interest!
